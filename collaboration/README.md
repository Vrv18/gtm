# Cross-Team Collaboration

Hub for managing initiatives that span multiple departments and fostering alignment across GTM functions.

## Collaboration Types

- **Cross-functional Projects** - Multi-department initiatives
- **Resource Sharing** - Shared assets, templates, and knowledge
- **Joint Planning** - Coordinated strategies and campaigns  
- **Knowledge Exchange** - Best practices and learnings

## Active Collaborations

Track ongoing cross-team work in the `active/` folder.

## Communication Channels

- **Weekly Sync:** [Day/Time] - All department heads
- **Monthly Review:** [Day/Time] - Full team collaboration review
- **Quarterly Planning:** Cross-team OKR alignment and planning
